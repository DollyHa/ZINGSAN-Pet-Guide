NFC Setup Guide

Follow these steps to program your dog's tag.

1 Get the App

Download the free NFC Tools app to your smartphone:

iOS App Store Google Play

2 Write Your Info

  1. Open the app and select Write.
  2. Tap Add a record -> Text.
  3. Enter info (e.g., Dog: Yelo | Ph: +1 000-000-0000).
  4. Tap Write and hold your phone near the tag.

3 Permanent Lock

Once verified, you can use the "Lock" feature in the app to prevent others from changing the info.

Warning: Locking is permanent. Please double-check your phone number!
